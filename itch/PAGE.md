# Clam Jumper — itch.io page copy

**Tagline:** Pick your hunter, crank clams open, and out-pearl the rival on the reef.

## Description

An ocean-bed take on the 1982 arcade game *Claim Jumper*: instead of a cowboy
racing a rival for gold bars, you're one of the reef's real clam specialists —
sea star, octopus, or ray — racing a rival one to crank pearls out of clams
scattered across a coral maze. Leave a clam half-open and the rival will come
jump your claim; a moray eel patrols the corridors to keep everyone honest.

Each hunter plays its own game. The sea star can't hop at all but clamps down
and never really loses progress; the octopus jet-dashes over anything and
inks its pursuers; the ray glides on held A and wing-slams clams part-open on
landing. The rival is a different species every reef and hunts like it —
dashing, farming, or gliding — with a Pearl Rain bonus round every second
reef and a sparse ocean groove that deepens as you go.

## Features

- Three playable species with distinct movement, pry profiles, and A-button
  abilities: clamp, dash + ink, hold-to-glide + wing-slam
- A rival that spawns as a random species other than yours and uses its own
  signature moves against you
- Crank-to-pry clams: the further you crank, the wider they gape
- Procedural connected coral mazes, deeper and denser every reef
- Moray eels that ignore ink and punish grounded critters
- Pearl Rain bonus round every second reef
- Vector-drawn wildlife, synth ocean groove, saved high score and species pick

## Controls

- **D-pad** — move
- **Crank** — pry the clam underfoot
- **A** — species ability (hold for the ray's glide); confirms menus
